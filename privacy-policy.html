Privacy Policy for M&M Fence Quote

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

1. Introduction

M&M Fence Quote ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use the M&M Fence Quote app (the "App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

M&M Fence Quote is designed with privacy in mind. All data you enter into the App is stored locally on your device.

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use the App, you may enter the following types of information:

  • Customer Information: Names, addresses, phone numbers, and email addresses of your customers
  • Project Details: Quote dates, installation dates, project specifications, and work descriptions
  • Financial Information: Pricing data, payment amounts, check numbers, and payment dates
  • Drawings and Images: Hand-drawn layouts and diagrams created within the App
  • Quote Data: Fence and gate segment details, materials, measurements, and installation specifications

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App does not automatically collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. We do not track your location, device information, or how you use the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

All information entered into the App is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage quotes for your customers
  • To generate PDF documents containing quote information
  • To store your quote history for future reference
  • To enable you to share quotes with your customers via PDF

Important: We do not access, collect, transmit, or store any of your data on our servers. All data remains on your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All data entered into the M&M Fence Quote app is stored exclusively on your iPad using iOS local storage (AsyncStorage). Your data is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

4.2 Data Security

Your data security depends on the security measures of your device:

  • Use device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID protection
  • Keep your iOS software up to date
  • Enable device encryption (enabled by default on iOS)
  • Back up your device regularly to prevent data loss

4.3 Data Backup

Your quote data will be included in your device's iCloud or iTunes backups if you have enabled device backups. This is controlled by your iOS settings, not by the App.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to your information.

5.2 PDF Sharing

When you generate and share a PDF quote from the App, you control who receives that document. The sharing functionality uses the standard iOS sharing capabilities, and any data shared is at your discretion.

5.3 Legal Requirements

Since we do not collect or have access to your data, we cannot be compelled to share it with law enforcement or other parties.

6. Children's Privacy

The M&M Fence Quote app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is designed for professional contractors and business use.

7. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We use only standard iOS frameworks for core functionality:

  • React Native: Mobile app framework
  • Expo: Development and build tools
  • AsyncStorage: Local data storage
  • expo-print: PDF generation (operates locally on device)
  • expo-sharing: Uses iOS native sharing capabilities

None of these services transmit your data to external servers.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the App
  • Modification: Edit any quote or information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual quotes or all data by deleting the App
  • Export: Generate PDF versions of your quotes

8.2 Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  1. Delete individual quotes within the App, or
  2. Uninstall the App from your device

Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data unless you have a device backup.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • Providing an in-app notification if changes are material

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

10. California Privacy Rights

Under California Civil Code Section 1798.83, California residents have the right to request information about the disclosure of personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Since we do not collect, store, or share your personal information with third parties, this right does not apply to the M&M Fence Quote app.

11. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not act as a data controller or processor, standard GDPR obligations do not apply to our App. You maintain full control and ownership of your data.

12. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as:

  • The App remains installed on your device
  • You choose to keep the individual quotes

You can delete individual quotes at any time or uninstall the App to remove all data.

13. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share a PDF quote.
